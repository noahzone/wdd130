Overview

Purpose

The purpose of creating a website for our rafting business is to inform people that we exist and book them as clients.

Audience

Our target audience includes adventurists, family reunions, and boy scouts. We cater to individuals aged 18-45 who are physically active and enjoy nature.

Scenario Questions:

  1. What does our target audience want?

    2. They seek outdoor activities that offer a break from their routine and allow them to reconnect with nature.

  2. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

    3. Answer: They seek clear pricing information, availability, and booking options.

  3. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?

    4. Answer: Mobile access is crucial, especially for individuals who might be on-the-go or planning activities while traveling.
